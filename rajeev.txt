Mean Relative error can misleads in these situations :
- Some actual values are very small
- There are few large outliers
- sometimes cells may not be comparable
         
Solution : 
- MAE and RMSE : it tells absolute size of the error
- median absolute percentage error(sMAPE)
- R square
- bias : check model is systemically over or under fitting

Good to go : 
MRE < 5% or sMAPE < 5%
RMSE and MAE are reasonable
R² > 0.95
bias is near zero
